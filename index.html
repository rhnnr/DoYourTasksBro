DoYourTasksBro

Less Talking, More Doing

This isn't your typical, bloated corporate calendar. DoYourTasksBro is an aggressive commitment dashboard built for raw execution. We don't care about your color-coded tags or deep multi-line descriptions. Here, your commitments are raw actions. Break your goals down, input them quickly, and destroy them immediately. The board is waiting.

Understand the Tiers

Every single character you type adds heavy physical weight to your gravity board. If your tasks get too wordy, or your backlog stacks too high, the system shifts states to guard your focus:

  • 🟢 Safe Zone (< 300 Points): Clear skies. Keep your velocity high and stack your cards.
  • 🟡 Warning Zone (300 - 600 Points): Pressure mounting. The interface turns hostile. Your commitments are becoming unmanageable.
  • 🔴 Critical Overload (600+ Points): TOTAL INPUT LOCKOUT. The app freezes your keyboard and disables your submission deck. You are banned from creating new tasks until you check off your current baggage.
